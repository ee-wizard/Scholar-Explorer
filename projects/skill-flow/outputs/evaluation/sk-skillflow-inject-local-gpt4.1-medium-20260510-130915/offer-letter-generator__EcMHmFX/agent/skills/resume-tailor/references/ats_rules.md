# ATS Compliance Rules for Resume Formatting

When generating the final resume content or advising on layout, STRICTLY ADHERE to these rules to ensure compatibility with Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

## 1. Structure & Hierarchy
- **Standard Headings:** Use standard section titles: "Experience", "Education", "Skills", "Projects". Avoid creative headers like "My Journey" or "Professional DNA".
- **Reverse Chronological:** Always list experience from newest to oldest.

## 2. Content Formatting
- **No Graphics/Images:** Do not include headshots, logos, or icons.
- **No Columns:** Use a single-column layout. Multi-column text often gets parsed out of order.
- **No Tables:** Do not use tables for layout; use tabs or lists.
- **Standard Bullets:** Use standard solid discs (•) or hyphens (-). Avoid arrows (➢) or checkboxes.

## 3. Font & Typography
- **Fonts:** Use standard sans-serif fonts (Arial, Calibri, Helvetica, Roboto) or serif fonts (Times New Roman, Georgia).
- **Size:** 10-12pt for body text, 14-16pt for headers.

## 4. Keywords
- **Exact Match:** Include keywords exactly as they appear in the Job Description (e.g., if JD says "React.js", do not just write "React").
- **Context:** Do not "keyword stuff" (hide white text). Keywords must appear naturally in bullet points or the Skills section.
